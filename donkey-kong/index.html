Donkey Kong

Description

Donkey Kong is a classic singleplayer platforming arcade game in which you play as Mario and ascend a construction site to rescue Pauline from the giant gorilla Donkey Kong, while he throws barrels and leaves other obstacles in your path.

Publishing Info

  • Published by: Nintendo
  • Developed by: Nintendo R&D1
  • Released: Oct, 1981

Game Categories

  • Genre: Action
  • Perspective: Side view
  • Visual: Fixed / flip-screen
  • Gameplay: Arcade, Platform
